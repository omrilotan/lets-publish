# Thank you for your contribution

Thank you for taking the time to contribute! I appreciate it.

## Make a Suggestion, Relay an Insight, Complain, Open an Issue

Anything you want to say is welcome - as long as you are considerate of other people and express yourself with dignity.

Please feel free to [open an issue](https://github.com/omrilotan/lets-publish/issues/new).

Specify NPM/NodeJS version, OS, how you installed the package, and any other information that might be relevant.
